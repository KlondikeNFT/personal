Crypto Made Simple
★ A Duolingo Inspired Course ★

More times than not, whenever I mention Bitcoin to someone, they tell me, “I dont understand crypto,” which is why I created this little course. I wanted to make it easy for anyone to learn about cryptocurrency. It’s not just one thing-It’s a mix of technologies and ideas. For instance, cryptography itself dates all the way back to 1900 BC and is actually used by us every single day when you go to unlock your iPhone! My goal is to break it all down so that even a child can understand. Dive in and explore the fascinating world of crypto with me!
